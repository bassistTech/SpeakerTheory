Basic Speaker Modeling Program

Revision date: March 24, 2026

This is a simple modeling program for basic sealed and ported speakers. It uses the formulas that I derived in my speaker theory notebooks.

A quick note about the Pe parameter. The program doesn't use it. Instead, the program uses the Amplifier Power parameter for the excursion and port air speed graphs. This lets you figure out how your speaker will perform for your amplifier.

How do I save or share my design?

It's right here. Your entire design is encoded in this URL. It will reproduce your design if you enter it into your browser. You can send this URL to a friend or save it in a file as way to "save" your design. This works without sharing any information with me. My web page does not gather or store your information.
